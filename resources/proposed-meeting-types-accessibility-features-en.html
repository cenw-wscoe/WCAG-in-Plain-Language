Proposed Meeting Types with Accessibility Features

General team/project meeting

Internal only

  • Recommendation: MS Teams
  • Apply all accessibility features necessary based on known audience or accommodation requests were done ahead of time.
  • This is a typical meeting for a team (usually less than 25 participants and all accommodations are known ahead of time)
  • This scenario is most likely 95%+ of all meetings held at ESDC

Internal and external – No protected information

  • Recommendation: MS Teams (recommended) or Zoom
  • Apply all accessibility features necessary based on known audience AND accommodation requests are done ahead of time
  • Zoom could also be used in this case depending on a variety of factors (amount of external participants and accommodation requests)

Internal and external – Protected information

  • Recommendation: MS Teams
  • Apply all accessibility features necessary based on known audience AND accommodation requests were done ahead of time

All-staff meeting

Internal and audience known

  • Recommendation: MS Teams
  • Apply all accessibility features necessary based on known audience AND accommodation requests were done ahead of time
  • If all-staff meeting is bilingual or French then CART will be required
  • Depending on accommodation responses then CART and/or Sign Language Interpretation (SLI) may be required
  • If SLI is required then switch to Zoom if no protected information. If protected information then ensure SLI workaround is in place prior to meeting.
    • SLI workaround for MS Teams is to ensure the required attendees have two devices to connect to the meeting, one to view the presentation, and one dedicated view for the SLI.

Special event

Internal - up to 325 participants

  • Recommendation: MS Teams
  • Ensure that accommodation requests are done ahead of time
  • Depending on accommodation responses then CART and/or SLI may be required
  • If event is bilingual or French then CART will be required
  • If SLI is required then switch to Zoom if no protected information. If protected information then ensure SLI workaround is in place prior to meeting.
    • SLI workaround for MS Teams is to ensure the required attendees have two devices to connect to the meeting, one to view the presentation, and one dedicated view for the SLI.

Internal – over 325 participants

  • Recommendation: MS Teams (Live events version)
  • Ensure that accommodation requests are done ahead of time
  • Depending on accommodation responses then CART and/or SLI may be required
  • If event is bilingual or French then CART will be required
  • If SLI is required then switch to Zoom if no protected information. If protected information then ensure SLI workaround is in place prior to meeting.
    • SLI workaround for MS Teams is to ensure the required attendees have two devices to connect to the meeting, one to view the presentation, and one dedicated view for the SLI.

Internal and external - over 325 participants - no protected information

  • Recommendation: Zoom
  • Ensure that accommodation requests are done ahead of time
  • Depending on responses then CART and/or SLI may be required
  • If event is bilingual or French then CART will be required

Consultations with Canadian citizens (no protected information)

  • Recommendation: Zoom
  • Ensure CART is available in both languages
  • Ensure SLI is available (not necessary if accommodation requests were made available prior)
